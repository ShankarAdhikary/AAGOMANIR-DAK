About the Event

Mahalaya marks the auspicious arrival of Maa Durga, a time when devotion, culture, and togetherness bloom. Through AAGOMANIR DAK, we aim to bring alive the spirit of Bengal in RRU with traditions, art, and joy.

“Your participation will give us happiness and make this celebration truly complete. Come, join us in welcoming Maa Durga with devotion and festivity!”
